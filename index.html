Cursor Tips & Shortcuts

Helpful commands to speed up your workflow

1

Open AI Chat

Press Cmd+L (Mac) or Ctrl+L (Windows) to open the AI chat panel.

2

Inline Edit

Select code and press Cmd+K to edit inline without opening the chat.

3

Reference Files

Use @filename in chat to reference other files in your project.

4

Accept Changes

Review AI suggestions in the diff view and press Cmd+Enter to accept.
