# Start Here

**Welcome!** You're about to build a web app with Cursor.

## 3 Steps to Get Started

### Step 1: Open Chat
Press `Cmd+L` (Mac) or `Ctrl+L` (Windows) to open the AI chat.

### Step 2: Say Hello
The AI will welcome you automatically. Or type `/welcome` for an introduction.

### Step 3: Set Up Your App
Type `/setup` and follow the guided wizard.

---

**That's it!** The AI will guide you from here.

## Quick Commands

| Command | What it does |
|---------|--------------|
| `/setup` | Configure your app |
| `/launch` | Start your app |
| `/help` | See all commands |
